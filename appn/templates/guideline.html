Guidelines for the Topic URL


1

Remove the https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/ part from the actual url

For eg.
Actual url >> https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/fundamentals-of-algorithms/

Topic URL >> fundamentals-of-algorithms/

2

Always choose pages with a lot of content links

3

Choose urls starting with https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/ and no other

4

Make sure that the links in the page refer to articles and not another set of links
